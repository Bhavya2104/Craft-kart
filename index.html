About us

We assist users from all around the country in discovering the origins of their cultural identity and customs through Craft & Kart. Our clients, particularly women, are given the opportunity to work toward achieving financial stability by either selling their goods or showcasing their talents with the intention of imparting information. At Craft & Kart, we take pleasure in our efforts to empower women by providing a platform on which they may advance their careers as they find and create employment.
